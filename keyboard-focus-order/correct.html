Correct: There is a logical tab order

Tab through the links on this page. The links above the heading are actually at the start of the tab order even though they come last in the HTML.

We could change how the tab index works by setting it to a value greater than 0. That would move it ahead of other elements on the page, but "Using a tabindex greater than 0 is considered an anti-pattern." Source: https://developers.google.com/web/fundamentals/accessibility/focus/using-tabindex
